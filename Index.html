Diet & Nutrition Plans

Fuel your body for optimal performance

Weight Loss Nutrition Plan

This plan is designed to help you lose fat while maintaining muscle mass through a moderate calorie deficit and balanced macronutrients. It focuses on whole, nutrient-dense foods that keep you full and energized throughout the day.

Protein

30%

Carbs

40%

Fats

30%

Sample Day Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: 3 egg whites + 1 whole egg, 1/2 cup oatmeal with cinnamon, 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • Mid-Morning Snack: 1 scoop protein powder with water, 1 small apple
  • Lunch: 4 oz grilled chicken breast, 1 cup roasted mixed vegetables, 1/2 cup quinoa
  • Afternoon Snack: 1/4 cup raw almonds, 1 string cheese
  • Dinner: 5 oz baked salmon, 2 cups steamed broccoli, 1 small roasted sweet potato
  • Evening Snack (optional): 1 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tbsp chia seeds

Muscle Gain Nutrition Plan

This plan provides a calorie surplus with high protein to support muscle growth and recovery from intense training. It includes nutrient timing strategies to maximize protein synthesis and muscle repair.

Protein

35%

Carbs

45%

Fats

20%

Sample Day Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: 3 whole eggs + 3 egg whites scrambled, 2 slices whole grain toast with 1 tbsp almond butter, 1 banana
  • Mid-Morning Snack: 1 scoop protein powder with 1 cup milk, 2 tbsp peanut butter on rice cakes
  • Lunch: 6 oz lean ground beef with 1 cup brown rice, 1 cup steamed vegetables with olive oil
  • Pre-Workout: 1 cup Greek yogurt with 1/2 cup granola and honey
  • Post-Workout: 2 scoops protein powder with 1 cup oats blended into a shake
  • Dinner: 8 oz grilled chicken breast, 2 cups whole wheat pasta with pesto, 1 cup roasted vegetables
  • Before Bed: 1 cup cottage cheese with 1 tbsp almond butter and cinnamon

Maintenance Nutrition Plan

This balanced plan helps you maintain your current weight while optimizing health and performance. It focuses on whole foods with a balance of macronutrients to support an active lifestyle.

30%

Carbs

Sample Day Meal Plan

  • Mid-Morning Snack: 1 scoop protein powder with water, 1 oz mixed nuts
  • Lunch: 5 oz grilled chicken, 1 cup quinoa salad with mixed vegetables and olive oil dressing
  • Afternoon Snack: 1 cup Greek yogurt with 1/4 cup mixed berries and 1 tbsp flax seeds
